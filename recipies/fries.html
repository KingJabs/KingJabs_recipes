Crispy French Fries

Description

These fries are golden crispy bois. They will taste good in your gullet

This quick easy fry recipe will knock you out of your socks!

Ingredients

  • Cooking oil of your choice
  • Potatoes cut into your prefered fry shape
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Cut potatoes into your prefered french fry shape.
  2. Soak fries in cool water for 30 minutes and then pat dry.
  3. Add oil and salt to fries.
  4. Cook for 12 minutes at 360f.
  5. Once fries have cooled eat them thangs
