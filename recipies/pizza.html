Pizza

Description

Pizza can be fun to make for the whole family. Consider making smaller pizas and allowing everyone to put on their own toppings.

This simple recipe is all you need to get started!

Ingredients

  • Dough
  • Pizza Sauce
  • Mozzerella
  • Toppings of your choice

Steps

  1. Shape dough into the pizza shape you want
  2. Put sauce on pizza
  3. put your toppings on the sauce
  4. put the cheese on your pizza
  5. cook pizza for 12 minutes at 450
  6. let pizza rest breifly then cut into slices and eat that thang
