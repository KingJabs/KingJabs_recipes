Stove Top Popcorn

Description

Making popcorn in a pot on the stove is quick and simple.

Follow this easy recipe to make the perfect popcorn every time.

Ingredients

  • Popcorn
  • High smoke point oil (like avacado oil)
  • Butter
  • Pop corn flavouring or salt

Steps

  1. Heat oil on medium for 2 minutes and add popcorn.
  2. Once popping slows dump popcorn into a bowl.
  3. Melt butter and pour on popcorn. Mix well so popcorn is evenly coated.
  4. Add salt or any flavouring you want to add. Make sure you mix well so flouring is even and then eat that thang
